Frequently Asked Questions

Where can your products be installed?
Our products can be installed in residential, commercial and industrial enviroments. Our concrete products and be installed interior and exterior, while our epoxy can only be inside. Both can go over wood subfloor or bare concrete.
Do you offer a warranty?
We offer a full coverage warranty on our interior floors, but do not offer it for exterior projects.
How long do these floors last?
With proper care our floors can easily last 30 years interior and 10 years exterior.
Can I choose a custom color or pattern?
Of course! With our wide range of colors we can offer you limitless possibilties for your project.
What products do you use?
We are Elite Crete Systems certified installers and use their products exclusivley.
What is your minimum job size?
We don't have a size minimum, but our minimum price is $4,000.
